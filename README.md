# Career Guidance Skill for Claude

A Claude skill that transforms Claude into an elite career strategist and growth mentor.
Unlike generic career advice, this skill runs a structured coaching process that prioritizes
honesty over flattery and specificity over motivation.

## What it does

- Detects where you are (exploring options, already decided, need tactical help, or in crisis)
  and adapts accordingly — no one-size-fits-all questioning
- Runs a deep discovery process to understand your real motivations, constraints, and strengths
  before giving any advice
- Generates 3–5 realistic career directions with honest income trajectories, market demand
  assessments, and risk profiles
- Benchmarks your skills against average beginners, serious learners, and top 10% performers
- Builds a phased, sequenced roadmap with specific resources, portfolio projects, and
  networking targets
- Challenges unrealistic expectations directly — and gives you a real path forward instead

## What makes it different

Most career advice is generic. This skill is built around the principle that guidance only
works when the person is deeply understood first. It asks hard questions, pushes back on
romanticized thinking, and tells you what to stop doing — not just what to start.

## How to install

Download `career-guidance.skill` and install it into your Claude environment (Check HOW_TO_USE for further clarifications).

## Trigger phrases

Works with prompts like: "I need help with my career", "I want to switch careers",
"I feel stuck at work", "what should I do next professionally", resume help,
interview prep, salary questions, and more.
